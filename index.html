About the Provost

Prof. Victor Folorunso Olaleye assumed office as the Provost of the Postgraduate College, Obafemi Awolowo University in 2021. His tenure was marked by visionary leadership, unwavering dedication, and impactful initiatives.

Notable among his achievements include organizing a health walk for postgraduate staff and leading the 60th Anniversary celebration of the Postgraduate College.
