Celebrating a Legacy of Leadership (2021 - 2025)

About the Provost

Prof. Victor Folorunso Olaleye served as the Provost of the Postgraduate College, Obafemi Awolowo University, from 2021 to 2025. A visionary academic leader, he brought energy, excellence, and compassion to the office throughout his tenure.

Under his leadership, the College experienced notable advancements — from initiating health awareness programs like the Health Walk for Staff, to coordinating the memorable celebration of the 60th Anniversary of the Postgraduate College.

Prof. Olaleye is widely recognized for his commitment to academic integrity, staff welfare, and community-building. His legacy is not only marked by achievements, but also by the warmth, wisdom, and humility with which he led.

As he transitions from this role, we celebrate a man who truly embodied service, scholarship, and impact.
